How to Place an Order

To place an order, simply search for a restaurant using the search bar, browse the menu, and add items to your cart. Once you're ready, click the cart icon and proceed to checkout. You'll need to sign in or create an account to complete your order.

Delivery Information

We offer delivery within a 10-mile radius of participating restaurants. Delivery times typically range from 30-60 minutes depending on your location and restaurant preparation time. Track your order status in the "Orders" section.

Contact Support

If you need assistance, our support team is available 24/7. Reach out via email at support@foodiefly.com or call us at 1-800-FOODIE (1-800-366-343). We're here to help with any issues or questions!
